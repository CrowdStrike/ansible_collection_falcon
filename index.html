Crowdstrike.Falcon

Collection version 4.11.0

  • Description

Description

The Falcon Ansible Collection serves as a comprehensive toolkit for streamlining your interactions with the CrowdStrike Falcon platform.

Author:

  • CRWD Solutions Architecture <integrations@crowdstrike.com>

Supported ansible-core versions:

  • 2.15.0 or newer
